Who We Are

We are a non-profit organization that operates in East Africa, focusing on Burundi. Founded on February 1, 2014, by Evangelist Dr. Ally Mfaume Miburo, along with:

William Pablo Babu, Justin Niyonkuru, Kizungu Rugamba Fabien, Lionceau Birikunzira, Saidi Niyonkuru, Samson Bigirimana

We serve as a center for international dialogue about the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and provide training in:

Entrepreneurship, Leadership, Awareness

BAC Youth Global strives to empower youth and underserved communities in the region, fostering creativity and innovation.
